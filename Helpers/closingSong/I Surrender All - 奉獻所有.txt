wǒ qiāng suǒ yǒu xiàn yú yē sū
我將所有獻與耶穌， 
gān xīn le yì quán fèng xiàn
甘心樂意全奉獻; 
wǒ yào shí cháng ài zhǔ kào zhǔ
我要時常愛主靠主， 
tiān tiān huó zài zhǔ miàn qián
天天活在主面前. 
quán suǒ yǒu fèng xiàn
全所有奉獻， 
quán suǒ yǒu fèng xiàn 
全所有奉獻
xiàn yú ài wǒ zūn guì jiù zhǔ 
獻與愛我尊貴救主
quán suǒ yǒu fèng xiàn
全所有奉獻
(English)
All to Jesus I surrender
All to Him I freely give
I will ever love and trust Him 
In His presence daily live Refrain:
I surrender all, I surrender all 
All to Thee my blessed Savior 
I surrender all

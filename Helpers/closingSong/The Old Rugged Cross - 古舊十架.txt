gè gè tā shān lǐng shàng
各各他山嶺上 ，
chù lì gǔ jiù shí jià
矗立古舊十架，
shì xiū rǔ yǔ tòng kǔ jì hào
是羞辱與痛苦記號, 
shén ài zǐ zhǔ yē sū
神愛子主耶穌， 
wèi wǒ men bèi dīng sǐ zhè shí jià
為我們被釘死，這十架 
shì wǒ zuì ài zuì bǎo
是我最愛最寶。 
gù wǒ ài gāo jǔ zhǔ shí zì jià
故我愛高舉主十字架，
yuàn jiāng shì shàng x ū róng quán fàng xià
願 將 世 上 虛 榮 全 放 下，
wǒ yī shēng yào bēi fù shí zì jià
我一 生要背負十字架，
dào nà tiān kě huàn gōng yì guān miǎn
到那天可換公義冠冕。
(English)
On a hill far away, 
stood an old rugged Cross
The emblem of suff'ring and shame
And I love that old Cross 
where the dearest and best 
For a world of lost sinners was slain
So I'll cherish the old rugged Cross
Till my trophies at last I lay down
I will cling to the old rugged Cross
And exchange it some day for a crown

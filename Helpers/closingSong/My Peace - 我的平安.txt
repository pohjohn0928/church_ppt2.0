wǒ qiāng píng ān cì gei nǐ
我將平安賜給你,
zhèi píng ān fēi shì jiè suǒ néng gei
這平安非世界所能給,
zhèi píng ān fēi shì jiè suǒ néng gòu liǎo jiě
這平安非世界所能够了解,
jiè tā zhī jiè tā huó
藉它知， 藉它活,
wǒ qiāng píng ān cì gei nǐ
我將 平安賜給你
(English)
My Peace
My Peace I Give Unto You; It ́s A Peace That The World Cannot Give;
Its A Peace That The World Cannot Understand,
Peace to Know, Peace To Live, My Peace I Give Unto You.
